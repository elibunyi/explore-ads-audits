P1 CONFIRMEDConversion Tag Broke on March 18 — Not a Google Ads Issue

Evidence: 8 conversions fired between 3/12-3/17 (6 days). Zero conversions from 3/18-3/23 (6 days). Both campaigns continued serving 63 clicks in the dark period at normal CPCs ($4-6 range). Click volumes and impressions are steady — only the conversion signal died.

What this means: The Google Ads campaigns are working correctly. Something on the website — GTM container, GA4 tag, or the conversion event itself — stopped firing around 3/17-3/18. This is a website/GTM issue, not a Google Ads configuration issue.

Pre-break CPA was $89.28 — healthy for this market. The $283 spent since the break is not "wasted" in the sense that clicks are still driving real visits; we just can't measure conversions.

For the Thursday meeting: Frame this as a tracking outage, not a performance decline. The Waco market was performing well post-change. Once the tag is fixed, the data will resume. Recommend manual form test + GTM container review as immediate actions.
