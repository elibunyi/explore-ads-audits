P2 — "Pool Contractor" Ad Group: $228, 41 Clicks, 0 Conversions — Low Intent Audience

Searches for "pool contractor" typically signal B2B or general contractor intent, not homeowner purchase intent. This audience is likely browsing, comparing prices, or looking for subcontractors — not requesting a quote. The $228 spend has yielded nothing measurable.

P2 — Max Clicks Strategy Blocks Conversion Optimization

Both campaigns run Target Spend (Max Clicks) with a $15 CPC ceiling. With 15 conversions confirmed over 30 days, there's now enough data to trial Maximize Conversions bidding. Max Clicks optimizes for traffic volume, not lead quality. The current CPA of $206 could likely improve with smart bidding. The $15 CPC ceiling is appropriate but conversion-focused bidding would direct clicks toward higher-intent users.

Action: Once non-converting ad groups are paused/reduced (cleaning up conversion signal), test Maximize Conversions on the Fiberglass Pools (Primary) campaign first. Monitor for 2-3 weeks before extending to Pool Builder campaign.
