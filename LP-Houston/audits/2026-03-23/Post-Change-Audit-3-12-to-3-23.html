CONFIRMED: Duplicate (Actually Triplicate) Conversion Tracking Is Active

The prior audit's finding about duplicate conversion tracking was correct and actually understated. This account has THREE layers of conversion tracking for the same user actions, all included in the "Conversions" metric simultaneously:

  • Layer 1 -- Website Tags (ONE_PER_CLICK counting): "Request Quote", "Download Brochure", "Contact Dealer"
  • Layer 2 -- GA4 Houston Property (MANY_PER_CLICK counting): "Contact Form", "Download Brochure", "Free Estimate" from GA4 property 512988705
  • Layer 3 -- GA4 USA Property (MANY_PER_CLICK counting): "free_quote_tx1", "contact_tx1", "get_brochure_tx1" from GA4 property 348960657

Impact: A single form submission could be counted up to 3 times. The reported 17 conversions in this window are likely 6--10 actual unique lead events. This is the root cause of the lead quality perception problem -- Donavan sees 42 "conversions" in 30 days but far fewer actual leads.
