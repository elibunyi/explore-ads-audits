High Priority Findings 3
High 19.77% Impression Share — 67.4% Lost to Rank Losing ~4 of every 5 eligible impressions

The campaign is being outranked on 67.4% of eligible impressions. With an actual avg CPC of ~$2.37 against a $3.50 CPC cap, this suggests either (a) ad quality is suppressing the effective rank, or (b) competition peaks above $3.50 during high-value queries and the cap cuts the account out of those auctions.

Max Clicks bidding with a $3.50 CPC cap on a $32.25/day budget is a conservative configuration. The cap needs to be removed or raised to $5–6 to remain competitive on high-intent queries in the Boston metro.

Action: Raise CPC cap to $5.00 or remove it entirely and let Max Clicks optimize freely. Monitor avg CPC for 2 weeks. Alternatively, switch to Target CPA bidding once sufficient conversion history (20+ conv) exists — this account is close.
