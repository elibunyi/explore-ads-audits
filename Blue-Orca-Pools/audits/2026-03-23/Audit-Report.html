Priority Findings — P1 (Critical)

P1 Single Ad Group — All 19 Keywords Lumped Together

The entire account runs through one ad group ("Ad group 1") with 19 keywords covering broad intent types including brand, generic installation, and product-type queries. This prevents theme-based Quality Score optimization, limits ad relevance scoring, and makes it impossible to segment budgets or bids by intent. This was flagged on the 3/20 audit and remains unresolved.

Impact: Lower Quality Scores → higher CPCs → poorer IS. $1,352 CAD/30d running through a single, unfocused ad group.

Fix: Restructure into at minimum 3 ad groups: (1) Fiberglass Pool Installation — core installation KWs, (2) Pool Type / Product — fiberglass, prefab, inground, (3) Brand — leisure pools, blue orca. Each ad group gets its own RSA with matching copy. Long-term: 5–6 tightly-themed groups.

Fix: Set path1 to Fiberglass-Pools and path2 to BC-Installer on current RSA. When restructuring ad groups, write theme-specific paths per ad group. Target EXCELLENT ad strength.
