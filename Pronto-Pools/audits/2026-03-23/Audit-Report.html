Campaign Structure

P2
Single-Ad-Group Architecture — All 23 keywords in one ad group

Everything lives in one ad group inside one campaign. This means one RSA serves all query intents simultaneously — brand queries, "pool companies" queries, "fiberglass pool installation" queries, and "swimming pool contractors" queries all getting the same ad. Google cannot optimize ad/keyword relevance across intents, degrading Quality Scores and CTR. Low QS on generic terms (pool companies QS 3, in ground pools QS 5, swimming pool installation QS 5) is partly structural.

Recommendation: Rebuild into 3–4 tightly themed ad groups: (1) Fiberglass-Specific [fiberglass pools, fiberglass pool installation, fiberglass pool cost], (2) Inground Pool General [inground pools, in ground pools near me, pool installation], (3) Pool Company/Builder [pool companies, pool builders, swimming pool contractors], (4) Design/Style [best swimming pool design, prefab pools, fiberglass pool shell]. Each ad group gets its own RSA with matching headlines.
P3
No Exact Match Keywords — Phrase-Only Coverage

All 23 keywords are PHRASE match (match type 3). While phrase is appropriate per account rules (no broad), exact match on proven performers would improve control over spend. "fiberglass pools", "pool installation", "fiberglass pool installation" are confirmed converters — exact versions would reduce wasted clicks on search term variants.

Recommendation: Add exact match versions of top converters: [fiberglass pools], [pool installation], [fiberglass pools near me], [fiberglass pool installation]. Keep phrase alongside. This improves quality score segmentation and bid control.

"leisure pools" Brand Keyword with QS 3

At a dealer account, brand keywords should be handled carefully to avoid cannibalization or brand confusion.

Recommendation: If the RSA doesn't have Leisure Pools brand mentions in headlines (unverifiable via API), the low QS is likely from a landing page mismatch. Verify LP and ad copy alignment. Consider whether this brand term should remain in a dealer account.
