Executive Summary

Jacksonville is one of the fleet's strongest performers — $44.62 CPA against a fleet average well above $70, with 37 conversions on $1,651 spend over the audit window. The account structure is clean: 2 active campaigns, 7 ad groups, solid negative keyword hygiene across 25–30 campaign-level exclusions, and confirmed shared set coverage on both campaigns.

One critical issue: "fiberglass pool builders" is running as BROAD MATCH in the Fiberglass Pool Installation ad group — a direct violation of the no-broad-match rule. Conversion rate on that keyword is also below account average.

Three quality score issues warrant attention: two QS=0 keywords and one at QS=3, all phrase-match in the Pool Builder campaign. These are actively suppressing impression share and dragging up CPCs.

What's working: Callout suite is strong (7 callouts per campaign), sitelinks are deployed on both campaigns, call extension present, and landing page performance is solid — the home and builder pages account for the top conversion volumes.
