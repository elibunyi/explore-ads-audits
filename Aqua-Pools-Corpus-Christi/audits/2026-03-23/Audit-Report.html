Conversion Tracking

Critical

🔴 ROOT CAUSE: Cross-Account Conversion Tracking Misconfiguration

customer.conversion_tracking_setting.conversion_tracking_status = 3 = CROSS_ACCOUNT.

This account is configured to use conversion tracking managed at a different account level (MCC or parent). This is architecturally correct for accounts managed under an MCC — however, it creates a critical dependency: if the cross-account tracking setup has any misconfiguration, GA4 property linking issue, or attribution error, this account receives zero conversion credit even when users complete form fills on the site.

The account has been running for 30 days with 210 qualified clicks (10% CTR, $6.17 CPC — both normal for this market) and has received zero tracked conversions. This is not consistent with normal pool lead generation; a typical 10% CTR pool campaign should yield 2–8% conversion rate on clicks to leads. At 210 clicks, the expected conversion range would be 4–17 form fills. Zero is a tracking failure.

Dealer must verify GA4 property 306536016 has active triggers on all Corpus Christi form pages. Check Google Ads link in GA4 admin. Confirm free_quote_request and conversion_event_submit_lead_form events are firing in GA4 DebugView when forms are submitted on aquapools.com/locations/corpus-christi/.
